Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Do you offer same day flower delivery in London?

A. We offer same day flowers London 7 days a week. Wherever you are in the capital we’ll make sure your flowers and plants are delivered on time.

Q. Do you deliver flowers and plants anywhere in the UK?

A. Yes, all our products and services are available throughout the UK. Choose your delivery options at checkout.

Q. Can I book late night delivery to London?

A. Yes. Late night delivery London is available whenever you need it. The service operations 7 days a week from 6pm-10pm.

Q. Do you offer Click & Collect service?

A. We do. Select the relevant option during checkout and we’ll update you when your order is ready. You can choose to collect your flowers from several of our partner shops as well as our main store in London. Please see options at the checkout or give us a call to find out more.

Q. What are your delivery charges?

A. We charge a flat rate of £6.95 for all same day and scheduled orders. Our special delivery options are charged at £9.95.

Q. What is the cut-off time for same day delivery of flowers and plants?

A. We have a cut-off time of 6pm for same day orders. Please complete your checkout process by this time to guarantee same day dispatch.

Q. Can we customize any of the products on your website?

A. Yes. Get in touch and we’ll be happy to customise any product for you.
